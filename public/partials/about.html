About This App

This app will allow you to easily keep track of expenses and income in order to create your own personal budget!

Log in is required for access. If you do not have an account please register now

How to Use the Finance App

The Accounts tab allows you to add different accounts such as a checking account or savings account. Once created press the details button to add individual transactions related to said account

Within the account details the History section will display a graph showing the detail of all transations

Use the categories tab in order to create or delete the categories that you would like to use to group your transactions
